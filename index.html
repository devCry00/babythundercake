How the contract works

When a sell occurs you will see on Poocoin chart 5 tx : one goes to seller, second one goes for 4% Cake rewards, third goes for 4% Poocoins rewards, fourth goes to liq 3% and last 5% to marketing so we can bring influencers on board. If you use dextools charts instead, you will only see one transaction.

8% Redistribution In THOREUM & CAKE 4% each respectively

8% of each purchase / sale is distributed among all holders that have at least 200,000 tokens. the rewards will be divided equally between Poocoin & Cake

Auto Paid Every 60 Minutes

The rewards autosystem is activated by volume every hour, your THOREUM & CAKE will automatically reach your wallet. If the volume is low, the transaction will be done when there is enough accumulation to do so to avoid gas fees.
